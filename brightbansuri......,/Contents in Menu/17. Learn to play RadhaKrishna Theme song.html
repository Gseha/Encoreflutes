Learn to play Radha Krishna Theme song



Here is the video tutorial. Watch it with pateince and enjoy playing Radha Krishna Theme Song.
Please Note that all the Swars used in this song are shuddh that is the normal S R G m, only in some lines a Komal n, r and g are used. Watch the tutorial for the instructions to play it.Also follow the Notation represenation chart in the Sargam represenation column.





Here are the Sargam Notation of the song for your reference.

If you want to know playing techniques please watch the video tutorial.
