Learn to play Jana Gana Mana


Are you excited to learn your first sond on your Bansuri?
Well I am excited too to teach you the song Jana Gana Mana.


We are clear with the concept of sargam and alankars, We just need to play combinations of sargam to make a song. We can make the song sound more beautiful by the use of Ornamentations that we will learn later.


Here is the video tutorial. Watch it with pateince and enjoy playing Jana Gana Mana.
Please Note that all the Swars used in this song are shuddh that is the normal S R G m, only in 2nd last line a Teevra Swar is used. Watch the tutorial for the instructions to play it.Also follow the Notation represenation chart in the Sargam represenation column.





Here are the Sargam Notation of the song for your reference.

If you want to know playing techniques please watch the video tutorial.
